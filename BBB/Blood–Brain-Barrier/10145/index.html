    BBB

    Definition : Blood–Brain Barrier
    Category : Medical » Anatomy & Physiology
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does BBB mean?

    Blood–Brain Barrier (BBB) is a highly selective semipermeable barrier running inside almost all vessels in the brain that lets through water, some gases and a few other select molecules, while preventing potentially toxic elements in the blood from entering the brain.
