    What does GST mean?

    Germanium-Antimony-Tellurium (GST or GeSbTe) is a phase-change material. Materials such as Germanium (Ge), Antimony (Sb), and Tellurium (Te) can be deposited onto substrates to form GeSbTe (GST) alloys.
