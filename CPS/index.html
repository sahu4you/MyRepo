    What does CPS mean?

    Current Population Survey (CPS) is a monthly survey of households conducted by the United States Census Bureau for the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS). It provides estimates and trends in employment, unemployment, earnings, hours of work, and other characteristics of the general labor force.
