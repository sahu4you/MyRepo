    DOWD

    Definition : Defendant/Offender Workforce Development
    Category : Governmental » Policies & Programs
    Country/Region : United States
    Popularity :

    What does DOWD mean?

    Defendant/Offender Workforce Development (DOWD) Conference is a unique event for anyone working with offender reentry. It has become a premier crime reduction and employment event in the United States.
