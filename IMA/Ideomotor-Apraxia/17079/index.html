    Definition : Ideomotor Apraxia
    Category : Medical » Diseases & Conditions
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does IMA mean?

    Ideomotor Apraxia (IMA) is a neurological disorder characterized by the inability to correctly imitate hand gestures and voluntarily pantomime tool use.
