    SIGBED

    Definition : Special Interest Group on Embedded Systems
    Category : Associations & Organizations » Technological Organizations
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does SIGBED mean?

    Special Interest Group on Embedded Systems (SIGBED) is a community of computer professionals to promote all aspects of embedded computing systems, including both software and hardware.
