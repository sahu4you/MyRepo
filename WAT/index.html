    WAT

    Definition : West Africa Time
    Category : Regional » Time Zones
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does WAT mean?

    West Africa Time (WAT) is a time zone used in west-central Africa. West Africa Standard Time is 1 hour ahead of Greenwich Mean Time (GMT+1)
