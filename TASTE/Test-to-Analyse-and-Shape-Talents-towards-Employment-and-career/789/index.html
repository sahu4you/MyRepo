    TASTE

    Definition : Test to Analyse and Shape Talents towards Employment and career
    Category : Academic & Science » Exams & Tests
    Country/Region : India
    Popularity :

    What does TASTE mean?

    Test to Analyse and Shape Talents towards Employment and career (TASTE) is a 4-Dimensional career profiling and mapping system developed by Astra HR Innovations Private Limited. TASTE targets school students from 9th standard to discover their most appropriate career. TASTE is based on psycho metrics for assessment.
