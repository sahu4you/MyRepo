    Definition : Peak Cell Rate
    Category : Computing » General Computing
    Country/Region : Worldwide

    What does PCR mean?

    Peak Cell Rate (PCR) is rate at which an Asynchronous Transfer Mode (ATM) endsystem can transmit cells into the network.
