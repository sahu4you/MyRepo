    Definition : Pyruvate Decarboxylase
    Category : Medical » Biochemistry
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does PDC mean?

    Pyruvate Decarboxylase (PDC) is a homotetrameric enzyme that catalyses the decarboxylation of pyruvic acid to acetaldehyde and carbon dioxide in the cytoplasm.
