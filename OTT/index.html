    What does OTT mean?

    Over-The-Top (OTT) refers to the delivery of content over the internet rather than through a traditional cable or broadcast provider. OTT rides on top of the pre-existing internet connection. Some examples of OTT services are Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, and so on.

    Note:
    OTT content is any content that comes from an OTT content provider.
    OTT device is a device used to consume OTT content. Examples: Apple TV, Chromecast, PlayStation, Xbox, Amazon Fire TV, and other streaming devices.
