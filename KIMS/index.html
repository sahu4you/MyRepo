    What does KIMS mean?

    Key Indicators Mapping System (KIMS) is a mapping software developed by World Agriculture Information Centre (WAICENT) for the specific purpose of mapping, collecting and disseminating food insecurity and vulnerability indicators that are relevant to Food Insecurity and Vulnerability Information and Mapping Systems (FIVIMS).
