    What does AICERA mean?

    Annual International Conference on Emerging Research Areas (AICERA) is an International Conference organized yearly by the Departments of Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & communication Engineering and Information Technology of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirappally, Kerala State, India. AICERA aims to bring engineers, academicians, scientists, industry researchers and students on a common platform, to exchange and share their experiences and research results on the various aspects of Computers and Communication Engineering.
