    RMB

    Definition : 人民币 Chinese [Ren-Min-Bi] [Renminbi]
    Category : Regional » Currencies
    Country/Region : China
    Popularity :

    What does RMB mean?

    Renminbi (Chinese: 人民币, Ren-Min-Bi, RMB, code: CNY) is the official currency of the People’s Republic of China. The main unit of the renminbi is the yuán. One yuan is divided into 10 jiǎo. One jiǎo is subdivided into 10 fēn.
