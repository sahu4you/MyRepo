    What does MLA mean?

    Mercury Laser Altimeter (MLA) is one of the instruments on MErcury Surface, Space ENvironment, GEochemistry, and Ranging (MESSENGER) spacecraft developed by National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). Mercury Laser Altimeter function was to provide detailed information regarding the topography and landforms of Mercury’s surface.
