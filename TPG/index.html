    What does TPG mean?

    Texas Pacific Group (TPG) a private equity and venture capital firm involved in incubation, early stage, post startup, growth capital, joint ventures, and large-stake purchases of companies. TPG invests in a broad range of industries including consumer/retail, media and telecommunications, industrials, technology, travel/leisure and health care.
