    TCC

    Definition : Total Cash Compensation
    Category : Business » Business Terms
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does TCC mean?

    Total Cash Compensation (TCC) is defined as the sum of base salary and short-term incentive received over an annual twelve month reporting period.
