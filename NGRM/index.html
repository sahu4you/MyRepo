    What does NGRM mean?

    The Narrow Gauge Railway Museum (NGRM) is a museum dedicated to narrow gauge railways situated at the Tywyn Wharf station of the Talyllyn Railway in Tywyn, Gwynedd, Wales, United Kingdom.
