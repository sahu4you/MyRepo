    Definition : Estimated Time of Arrival
    Category : Transport & Travel » Air Transport
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does ETA mean?

    Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA) is the date and time at which an air, ship, or a vehicle is expected to arrive at named city or port.
