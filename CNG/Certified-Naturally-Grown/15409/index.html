    CNG

    Definition : Certified Naturally Grown
    Category : Associations & Organizations » Regional Organizations
    Country/Region : United States
    Popularity :

    What does CNG mean?

    Certified Naturally Grown (CNG) is a farm assurance program certifying produce, livestock, and apiaries for organic producers who sell locally and directly to their customers in the United States.
