    Definition : Australian Sheep Veterinarians
    Category : Associations & Organizations » Animal Welfare
    Country/Region : Australia
    Popularity :

    What does ASV mean?

    Australian Sheep Veterinarians (ASV) is a special interest groups of the Australian Veterinary Association dedicated to sharing information with and educating members about issues that affect the production and health of sheep.
