    EMC

    Definition : Electromagnetic Compatibility
    Category : Academic & Science » Electrical
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :

    What does EMC mean?

    Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC) is the ability of an Electrical and Electronic (E&E) equipment to operate within its Electromagnetic (EM) environment and with other equipment without causing electromagnetic interference to other equipment or suffering unacceptable degradation in output or performance.
