    IMK

    Definition : In My Knowledge
    Category : Miscellaneous » Chat slang
    Country/Region : Worldwide
    Popularity :
    Type :
    Initialism

    What does IMK mean?

    IMK means “In My Knowledge”. It is used when you want to state that you are pretty sure about something, but you are not 100% sure that you are right.

    Note:
    Sometimes depending on the context, IMK also means “I Must Know”.
