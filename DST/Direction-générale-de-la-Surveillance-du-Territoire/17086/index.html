    DST

    Definition : Direction générale de la Surveillance du Territoire French [General Directorate for Territorial Surveillance]
    Category : Governmental » Security & Defence
    Country/Region : Morocco
    Popularity :

    What does DST mean?

    General Directorate for Territorial Surveillance (French: Direction générale de la Surveillance du Territoire, DST or DGST) is the internal intelligence agency of Morocco.
