    What does PPR mean?

    Pipe Penetrating Radar (PPR) is an application of Ground-Penetrating Radar (GPR) technology applied in underground utility pipes where the signals are directed through pipe and conduit walls to detect pipe wall thickness and voids behind the pipe walls.
